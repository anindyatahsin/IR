Final Glance: Autos companies

General Motors Co. rose $.06 or .2 percent, to $28.10.
                  
Honda Motor Co. rose $.33 or .8 percent, to $39.52.
                  
Toyota Motor Corp. rose $.28 or .3 percent, to $103.15.
