Midday Glance: Autos companies




NEW YORK (AP) -- Shares of some top autos companies are up at 1 p.m.:
                  
Ford Motor Co. rose $.13 or .9 percent, to $13.29.
                  
General Motors Co. rose $.70 or 2.5 percent, to $28.80.
                  
Honda Motor Co. rose $.18 or .5 percent, to $39.70.
                  
Toyota Motor Corp. rose $.69 or .7 percent, to $103.84.
