Early Glance: Beverages companies




NEW YORK (AP) -- Shares of some top beverages companies are up at 10 a.m.:
                  
Anheuser-Busch Inbev ADR rose $.70 or .7 percent, to $95.98.
                  
Coca-Cola Co. rose $.29 or .8 percent, to $39.63.
                  
Dr Pepper Snapple rose $.42 or .9 percent, to $45.40.
                  
Molson Coors Brewing Co. rose $.54 or 1.1 percent, to $48.37.
                  
PepsiCo Inc. rose $.17 or .2 percent, to $76.84.
